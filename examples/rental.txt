## RENTAL TERMS

**Monthly Rent:** $1,200.00
**Due Date:** 1st day of each month
**Late Fee:** $50.00 if rent is more than 5 days late
**Security Deposit:** $1,200.00 (refundable upon lease termination, subject to property condition)

**Utilities Included:** Water and trash collection
**Utilities NOT Included:** Electricity, gas, internet, cable

## TENANT OBLIGATIONS

1. **Rent Payment:** Tenant agrees to pay rent in full by the 1st of each month. Late payments will incur a $50 late fee after a 5-day grace period.

2. **Property Care:** Tenant must maintain the property in good condition, report any damages immediately, and not make alterations without written landlord consent.

3. **Occupancy:** Maximum of 2 persons may occupy the premises. No subletting without landlord's written permission.

4. **Noise:** Tenant agrees to maintain reasonable noise levels and respect neighbors, especially between 10 PM and 7 AM.

5. **Pets:** No pets allowed without prior written consent. If approved, additional $300 pet deposit required.

6. **Smoking:** Smoking is prohibited inside the premises and within 25 feet of the building.

## LANDLORD OBLIGATIONS

1. **Habitability:** Landlord will maintain the property in habitable condition and comply with all local housing codes.

2. **Repairs:** Landlord is responsible for major repairs and maintenance of heating, plumbing, and electrical systems.

3. **Notice for Entry:** Landlord will provide 24-hour written notice before entering the premises, except in emergencies.

4. **Security Deposit Return:** Security deposit will be returned within 30 days of lease termination, minus any deductions for damages beyond normal wear and tear.

## IMPORTANT CLAUSES

**Early Termination:** If tenant terminates lease early, they forfeit the security deposit and are liable for rent until a replacement tenant is found or lease expires, whichever comes first.

**Renewal:** This lease automatically converts to month-to-month after the initial term unless either party provides 30 days written notice of termination.

**Default:** If tenant fails to pay rent or violates any lease terms, landlord may terminate the lease with 30 days written notice (or shorter period as allowed by law).

**Dispute Resolution:** Any disputes will first be addressed through mediation. If unsuccessful, disputes will be resolved in the courts of Sangamon County, Illinois.

## ADDITIONAL TERMS

- Tenant is responsible for changing locks if desired (landlord must receive copies of all keys)
- Snow removal from walkways is tenant's responsibility
- Trash must be placed at curb on designated pickup days only
- Parking space #12 is included with this lease
- No washing machines or dishwashers may be installed without landlord consent

## TERMINATION CONDITIONS

Either party may terminate this lease by providing 30 days written notice. Tenant must return all keys and leave the property in clean condition, normal wear and tear excepted.

**Property Inspection:** A move-out inspection will be conducted within 3 days of lease termination to assess any damages.
